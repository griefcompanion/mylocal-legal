Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

MyLocal ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the MyLocal mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and any profile information you choose to provide, such as display name, profile photo, and selected regions.

Location Information

Location access is only requested when a vendor chooses to go live and share their current location. MyLocal does not track background location and does not continuously monitor user location.

User Content

We collect information you voluntarily provide, including vendor profiles, event listings, photos, and descriptions.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, we collect and store a device push token to send relevant alerts (such as vendor activity or event updates). You may disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Usage Data

We may collect limited technical information such as device type, operating system, and app performance data to improve functionality.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the MyLocal platform
  • To display vendor and event listings
  • To send notifications you have opted into
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To prevent fraud and maintain security

Third-Party Services

MyLocal uses third-party services to operate the app, including:

  • Firebase (authentication, database, storage, cloud functions)
  • Expo (app infrastructure and notifications)

These services may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with third-party service providers necessary to operate the application.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Choices

  • You may update or delete your profile information at any time.
  • You may disable push notifications in your device settings.
  • You may request account deletion by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

MyLocal is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
